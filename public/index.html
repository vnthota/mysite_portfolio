About Me A brief intro

I'm obsessed with technology, beautiful design and great products. After graduating from Portland State University, I was lucky enough to join the design consultancy firm, Ziba Design. There I fell in love with innovation in design, branding and creating "beautiful experiences." After leaving the US, I joined a local web startup in Macau where I worked for 6 months, initially heading social media and setting up technology standards. After a month I took on the responsibility of various other functions from website design & user experience to marketing & advertising campaigns. However the startup failed to launch; but the lessons learnt were invaluable.

I am now pursuing my passion for web design and development, focusing on learning and building creative & professional applications and products with the help from some great mentors and friends. My interests however, extend beyond the web as I am passionate about helping people create a brand, an identity and a marketing strategy for their business.

When I'm not engaged in long hours of creative outbursts, I work out, read, find time to travel for music/food/sports, listen to great electronic music and indulge in various technology gadgets and their applications.

I listen. I learn. I implement.
